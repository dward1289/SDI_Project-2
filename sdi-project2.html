SDI 1205: Devona Ward

Project 2

Jay-Z, Drake, and J Cole are some of my favorite rappers. If they really did have a concert together, I would definitely attend. I created a script based on my dream concert. Each artist is introduced to the stage, and their time is being counted in sets of 15. The total crowd was calculated as 120,000 people per singer. It was a full house. Everyone had a great time, and everyone enjoyed the artists.

May 9, 2012, Devona Ward
